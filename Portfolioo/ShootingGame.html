Shooting Game





Action and adventure games have always been my favorite types of games whether it be GTA, Watch Dogs, Sleeping Dogs, etc. Those games been a major part of my childhood and helped me have fun.


I thought it would be interesting to experiment with Javascript, specifically P5.js, to create a browser shooting game.


The game has two different environemnts and two weapons. You're given the choice of the environment first and then you get to choose your weapon.


There isn't a major difference betweent the weapons in terms of functionality, however, each enviornemnt is different:



•The first one has two players appearing and hiding from behind boxes in the game and it's up to you to shoot them because if you don't they will start shooting and eating up all 10 of your lives.







•The second environment has 4 different targets appearing and disappearing at different speeds meaning that some targets will stay on the screen for longer. hiting each target gives the user a point/kill. The second enviroment is just for fun so there isn't really winning or losing.
